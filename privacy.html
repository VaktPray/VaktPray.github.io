VaktPray Privacy Policy

VaktPray is designed to provide prayer times without collecting personal data, using analytics, or tracking you.

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Information the app collects

VaktPray does not collect personal data from the app. It does not use analytics, advertising identifiers, tracking SDKs, or third-party advertising services.

Prayer time data is included in the app, so no internet connection is required after installation.

Information stored on your device

VaktPray stores app preferences locally on your device, including your selected city, language, notification settings, notification sound choices, and display preferences. Some preferences are stored in the app's private shared storage so VaktPray widgets can show the same settings.

These preferences are not sent to VaktPray. They remain on your device unless you change them, reset them, or delete the app.

Location

VaktPray does not request precise device location. Prayer times are based on the city you select in the app.

Notifications

Prayer reminders are scheduled locally on your device through Apple's notification system. Notification permission is controlled by iOS and can be changed at any time in the Settings app.

Support email

If you email VaktPray support, your email address and the information you choose to include in your message are used only to respond to your request, bug report, or feedback.

Privacy choices

Because VaktPray does not collect personal data from the app, there is no account data to access, export, or delete. You can remove locally stored app preferences by deleting the app from your device.

Changes

If VaktPray's privacy practices change, this policy will be updated before those changes are released.

Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, bug reports, or feedback, email show support email.
